Privacy Policy for Power Prompts

Last Updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

Power Prompts is designed to help users access curated prompts that improve interactions with AI tools. Your privacy is important, and this app is built with a minimal data approach.

Power Prompts does not collect, store, transmit, or share personal data.

Information Collection

Power Prompts does not collect personal information.

  • No account creation is required
  • No user tracking
  • No analytics collection
  • No access to contacts, photos, or personal files

Any prompts or text you interact with remain on your device.

Data Storage

Power Prompts does not store user data on external servers. All content in the app is either preloaded prompt content included in the application or user text that remains locally on your device.

The app simply allows users to copy prompts for use with external AI tools.

Third-Party Services

Power Prompts does not integrate with external APIs, AI services, or third-party analytics providers.

The app does not transmit prompts or user content to any external service.

Data Security

Because Power Prompts does not collect or transmit user data, there is no external storage of personal information associated with app usage.

Children’s Privacy

Power Prompts does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect improvements to the app or changes in applicable policies. Updates will be posted with a revised “Last Updated” date.
